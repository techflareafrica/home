Before You Send

Quick answers to common contact questions.

How quickly will I receive a reply?

Response times depend on the volume and type of enquiry. Clear messages with a specific subject are usually easier to review.

Can I suggest an article topic?

Yes. Choose “Story suggestion” in the form and explain the topic, why it matters and the audience that would benefit.

Can I report an error in an article?

Yes. Select “Correction or feedback” and include the article title, the section concerned and the correct information where possible.
